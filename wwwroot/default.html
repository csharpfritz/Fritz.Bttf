Test Page

Proof of concept HTML for Marty McFly's time machine's date readout. All dates are from the second movie, with Biff just arriving in 2015 and Marty preparing to head back to 1985

In assembling this proof-of-concept, we learned that the digit 1 with the digital font is aligned left and does not format correctly with the shadow font. Each 1 is prefixed with a non-breaking space, "&nbsp;" in order to align the digits with the shadow 8 digits behind them.
